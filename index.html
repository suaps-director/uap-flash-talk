UAP Flash Talk

A short presentation on Unidentified Aerial Phenomena

Presented by Michael Cifone

What are UAPs?

  • Unidentified Aerial Phenomena.
  • A term used to describe unusual objects in the sky.
  • Often associated with military sightings and reports.

Key Observations

  • High-speed, unconventional maneuvers.
  • Lack of visible propulsion systems.
  • Trans-medium travel (moving between air and water).

Summary

The phenomenon of UAPs is a topic of serious discussion in scientific and governmental communities. Further investigation is needed to understand their nature.
